# Formspree Setup Guide (Super Simple!)

This is the easiest way to make your contact form work. Takes 2 minutes.

## Quick Setup (2 Steps)

### Step 1: Create Free Account
1. Go to [https://formspree.io/](https://formspree.io/)
2. Click "Sign Up" (free account = 50 submissions/month)
3. Verify your email

### Step 2: Get Your Form Endpoint
1. After signing up, you'll see a form endpoint like: `https://formspree.io/f/xxxxxxxxxx`
2. Copy that entire URL

### Step 3: Update Your Code
1. Open `script.js`
2. Find line ~75 where it says: `'https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID'`
3. Replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` with your actual form endpoint

## That's It!

Your form will now send emails directly to the email you used to sign up for Formspree.
